Wenge Xu | 徐温格

I'm a lecturer in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) based within the Human-Computeer Interaction Research Centre , Birmingham City University. My university profile can be found here page.


I obtained my PhD degree in 2021 from University of Liverpool, where I was supervised by Prof Hai-Ning Liang. Currently, my main research interests are: HCI, Extended Reality, Games (see my Google Scholar). My research mainly focuses on (1) understanding human factors of people interacting with emerging technologies and, (2) designing interaction for people interact with emerging devices.


Current projects are: (1) Inclusive eHMI for d/Deaf and hard-of-hearing people and (2) Inclusive VR games for d/Deaf and hard-of-hearing people.
